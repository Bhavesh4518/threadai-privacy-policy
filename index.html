ThreadAi - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 1 October, 2025

ThreadAi is designed to help users summarize online threads (Twitter/X, Reddit, forums, blogs) into concise summaries and generate human-like replies. We respect your privacy and handle your data responsibly.

1. Data We Collect

  • Website content: Visible text on supported sites is temporarily accessed to generate summaries and replies. No data is stored, logged, or shared with third parties. All processing occurs in real-time within your browser.
  • Preferences: We store your extension settings (such as summarization style, reply tone, or theme) locally on your device using Chrome’s local storage. This data never leaves your computer or device.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

We are committed to minimizing data collection. We do not collect:

  • Personally identifiable information (PII) such as name, email, IP address, or username.
  • Financial, health, biometric, or authentication data.
  • Your browsing history, search queries, or navigation patterns.
  • Location data or device identifiers.
  • Private messages, direct communications, or non-public content.

3. Payments

All payments and subscription management are securely handled by Lemon Squeezy, a PCI-DSS compliant payment processor. ThreadAi does not process, store, or have access to any credit card or financial information.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or transfer your data to any third parties. When generating summaries or replies, we send only the visible text content you select to our secure AI API provider (e.g., OpenAI or similar). This data is used solely for the purpose of generating your requested output and is deleted immediately after processing. No logs are retained.

Privacy by Design

ThreadAi operates entirely within your browser. Nothing is uploaded to our servers unless you explicitly request a summary or reply. Even then, data is transient and encrypted in transit. We prioritize on-device processing wherever possible.

5. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • You may uninstall the ThreadAi Chrome Extension at any time — this immediately deletes all locally stored preferences.
  • You may disable the extension without uninstalling to pause all functionality.
  • You can review or clear Chrome storage manually via: chrome://extensions/ → “Details” → “Site Data”.
